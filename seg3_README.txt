## Description of Triangle Role in part 3 of the project.

Triangle: Create a draft presentation to share with your class.

### Slides
Presentations are drafted in Google Slides.

### Content
The presentation tells a story about the project. As you draft your slides, be sure to address the following:

1. Project topic.
2. Reason topic was selected.
3. Description of your data and where it was sourced
4. Questions the team hopes to answer with the data.
5. Description of the data exploration your team conducted.
6. Description of the analysis conducted on the data.
7. Recount of the different technologies, tools, languages, and algorithms used throughout the project.
